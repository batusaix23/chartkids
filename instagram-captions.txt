👶 What's the BEST age for reward charts?

Research shows: 3-12 years old! 📊

Here's why:

💒 Ages 3-5: Simple charts with stickers & small rewards. They LOVE the visual stars! ⭐

👦 Ages 6-8: Can handle more tasks and understand weekly goals. Perfect for chores + homework!

👧 Ages 9-12: Ready for money-based rewards and longer-term goals. Great for teaching responsibility!

The key? Make it age-appropriate and FUN! 🎉
